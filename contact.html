❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Booking
How far in advance should I book? We recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance for peak season (Oct–Nov, March–May). Last minute is sometimes possible in low season.
Do I need travel insurance? Yes — travel insurance including helicopter evacuation cover is strongly recommended for trekking, especially at high altitude.
What is the group size? We cater to solo travellers, couples, families, and groups. Private and shared group tours are both available.
Can I customise my trip? Absolutely! All our itineraries are fully customisable. Just mention your preferences in the contact form above.
