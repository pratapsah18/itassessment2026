Welcome to Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country located between India and China. It has tall mountains, green forests, and historic towns to explore. Whether you like adventure, culture, or just relaxing, Nepal has something for everyone. Places like Pokhara and Kathmandu show the natural beauty and traditions of the country. Nepal is a place where nature, history, and friendly people come together to make every visit special.

Why Visit Nepal?

Nepal is home to 8 of the world's 14 highest peaks, over 800 species of birds, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, and some of the most welcoming people on the planet.
