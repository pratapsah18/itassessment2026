🏃 Adventure Activities

Nepal is the adventure capital of Asia — from sky-high trekking to roaring river rapids, there's something for every thrill-seeker.

What Can You Do in Nepal?

Pick your adventure and we'll help you plan the perfect trip

Most Popular

🏔 Trekking & Hiking

Nepal is the world's premier trekking destination. Famous routes include the Everest Base Camp trek, the Annapurna Circuit, Langtang Valley, and the Manaslu Circuit. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned trekker, there's a trail for you.

Duration: 3 days to 3 weeks
Difficulty: Easy to Extreme

Must Try

🍀 Paragliding

Pokhara is one of the best paragliding destinations in the world. Fly over Phewa Lake with stunning views of the Annapurna range. Tandem flights are available for beginners — no experience needed!

Duration: 20–40 minutes
Location: Sarangkot, Pokhara

Thrilling

🌊 White Water Rafting

Nepal's wild rivers offer some of the best rafting in the world. The Trishuli, Bhote Koshi, and Sun Koshi rivers provide exhilarating rapids ranging from Grade II to Grade V. Multi-day rafting trips are also available.

Best season: Sept–Nov, March–May
Difficulty: Medium to Hard

Unique Experience

✈️ Mountain Flight

Not ready to trek but want to see Everest up close? Take a 1-hour scenic mountain flight from Kathmandu. You'll fly alongside the entire Himalayan range and see Everest, Kanchenjunga, and other peaks from above the clouds.

Duration: 1 hour
Departures: Daily from Kathmandu TIA

Wildlife

🐖 Jungle Safari

Explore Chitwan or Bardia National Park on a jeep safari or elephant-back ride. Spot rhinos, tigers, leopards, crocodiles, and hundreds of bird species in their natural habitat. A truly unforgettable wildlife experience.

Best season: Oct–March
Locations: Chitwan, Bardia

Active

🚲 Mountain Biking

Pedal through ancient villages, terraced rice fields, and dense forests. The Kathmandu Valley offers excellent biking routes to Nagarkot, Dhulikhel, and Namobuddha. More challenging routes go through the Mustang and Manang valleys.

Best for: All fitness levels
Rental: Available in Kathmandu & Pokhara
