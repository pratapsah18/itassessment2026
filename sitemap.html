Website Structure

Navigate to any page on our site from here

  • 🌎 ExploreNepal – Main Website
    • 🏠 Home (index.html) — Welcome page with image slider, intro to Nepal, top destination previews, quick facts table, and external links.
    • 🏔 Destinations (destinations.html) — Detailed pages on Everest Region, Pokhara, Chitwan National Park, and Kathmandu Valley with a comparison table.
    • 🏢 Culture & Festivals (culture.html) — Nepal's religions, the Newari civilisation, major festivals (Dashain, Tihar, Holi), and traditional foods.
    • 🏃 Activities (activities.html) — Trekking, paragliding, white water rafting, mountain flights, jungle safari, and mountain biking.
    • 📚 Travel Tips (travel-tips.html) — Visa & entry requirements, best seasons to visit, daily budget guide, and packing essentials.
    • 📷 Gallery (gallery.html) — 10 photo thumbnails of Nepal linking to full-size images. Covers mountains, temples, wildlife, and landscapes.
    • 🗺️ Site Map (sitemap.html) — This page. A full overview of all pages on the website.
    • ✉️ Contact (contact.html) — Book a trip enquiry form with JavaScript validation, contact info, social links, and FAQ table.
